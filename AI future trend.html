The Twin Engines of Change: AI & Green Energy

The future is being reshaped by two great pillars: the intelligence revolution of AI and the transition to green energy. These twin forces interact and influence each other, fundamentally transforming industry, the economy, and our lives.

The Evolution Trajectory of AI

Beginning with the widespread use of multimodal AI, agentic AI capable of autonomous decisions will proliferate. By 2030, AI will be established as a common utility like electricity, holding a potential productivity value of $4.4 trillion.

Key Energy Transition Milestones

The energy transition accelerates in 2025 as renewables surpass coal. Solar and wind will overtake nuclear by 2026, and by 2029, solar will become the largest renewable source, powering 46% of the grid by 2030.

A Turbulent Economy and Labor Market

Major Economies Growth Rate Forecast (2025-2028)

The global economy faces a gentle slowdown, with AI's productivity effects gradually appearing. After initial struggles, the Korean economy will seek a tech-driven recovery.

The Future of Work: A Shifting Job Landscape

AI and automation are fundamentally reshaping the job market. Demand for new roles like AI specialists and green tech engineers will explode, while routine jobs decline, making the "adaptability gap" a central challenge.

The Rise of Physical AI: The Age of Robots

Beyond the digital space, AI is beginning to interact with the physical world. Humanoid and service robots will gradually expand from manufacturing and logistics into our daily lives.

Humanoid & Personal Robot Market Outlook

With mass production starting in 2026, humanoid robots will become a key industrial driver by 2030, improving productivity by up to 20-30%.

The market for personal assistant and service robots is projected to grow explosively, reaching approximately $63.8 billion by 2030.
